About Finhub

Finhub is a web based platform which helps university students budget their monthly income, calculate and tracks their monthly expenditure and makes sure that they are not overspending their money. This lessens cases of Students Loan Debt crisis. We hope that with our platform, students can be able to pay their tuition, rent etc without loans and all by themselves.
